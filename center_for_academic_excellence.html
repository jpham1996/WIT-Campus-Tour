The Center for Academic Excellence (CAE) is available to assist all Wentworth students with academic challenges in the areas of math, science, computer programs, technical courses specific to majors, and communications and writing. The CAE is a free resource for students looking to improve or maintain their academic standing. In this student-based learning environment, students can receive individual help with their studies, meet and work in study tables, or go online to find resources to assist them in meeting their goals for academic success.
